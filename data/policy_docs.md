# Lending & Credit Policy Documents

## Policy 1: Credit Score Requirements
Minimum bureau score must be 600 for basic approval. Scores above 750 are considered excellent and eligible for premium terms.
New to credit (NTC) applicants require additional documentation and stricter income verification.
Bureau score is the most critical factor in loan approval decisions.

## Policy 2: Income Verification and FOIR
Primary applicant monthly income must be verified through banking records and GST filings.
Minimum monthly income required is 25,000 for loan approval consideration.
FOIR (Fixed Obligation Income Ratio) should not exceed 0.5 for approval. FOIR above 0.6 indicates financial stress.
Debt to Income ratio should be less than 0.4 for safe lending approval.

## Policy 3: Business Vintage and Stability
Business should be operational for minimum 1 year. Preferred minimum vintage is 3 years.
New businesses (< 1 year) require additional collateral and guarantor support.
Business vintage of 5+ years indicates mature stable business operations.
GST registration age is a key indicator of legitimate business operations.

## Policy 4: Default Management and Credit History
Any wilful default flag will result in automatic rejection. Wilful default indicates intentional non-payment.
Overdue amount should not exceed 10% of average monthly turnover. High overdue indicates cash flow issues.
DPD (Days Past Due) >= 90 indicates high risk and potential default. DPD 30-60 requires careful review.
Active credit accounts should be > 2 to show healthy credit history and management capability.

## Policy 5: GST Compliance and Tax Filing
All businesses must have valid GST registration. Unregistered businesses are higher risk.
Minimum 12 months of GST filing history required. More than 24 months filing is preferred.
Missed returns > 3 in last year indicates compliance issues and potential tax evasion.
GST turnover and filing amounts should match with banking records for consistency verification.

## Policy 6: Banking Behavior and Liquidity
Average bank balance (ABB) should be >= 50% of 6-month average monthly turnover.
Bounce history (inward/outward) must be clean. More than 2 bounces in last 6 months is concerning.
Total credits and debits show business transaction volume. Higher volumes indicate stronger business.
Monthly turnover average and growth rate indicate business health and scalability potential.

## Policy 7: Lending Approval Threshold
Approval threshold: Bureau Score >= 600, Business Vintage >= 1 year, FOIR <= 0.5, No wilful default
Preference threshold: Bureau Score >= 700, Business Vintage >= 3 years, Monthly Income >= 100000
Premium tier: Bureau Score >= 750, Business Vintage >= 5 years, FOIR <= 0.3
